

# 🐾 Tail Wag (My_pet_Site)

Tail Wag is a community-driven platform for pet lovers to share stories, connect with other owners, and celebrate the joy of having pets. Whether you’re a dog person, cat enthusiast, or simply an animal admirer, Tail Wag provides a welcoming space for pets to shine.

---

## ✨ Features
- **Pet Profiles** – Create personalized pages for your pets with photos, fun facts, and milestones.  
- **Community Sharing** – Post stories, tips, and adorable moments to inspire and connect.  
- **Health & Care Guides** – Access reliable resources on nutrition, grooming, and training.  
- **Events & Activities** – Stay updated on pet meetups, adoption drives, and local happenings.  
- **Interactive Features** – Like, comment, and engage with fellow pet lovers.  

---

## 🎯 Mission
To celebrate the unconditional love pets bring into our lives while fostering a supportive community for owners to learn, share, and grow together.
